Find out what information the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency (DVLA) holds about a vehicle by searching on its registration number (number plate). With this service you can check details about a vehicle including its tax, SORN status, MOT expiry, date of registration, V5C issue date, year of manufacture, type approval category, weight, engine size, fuel type, emissions and export status.
