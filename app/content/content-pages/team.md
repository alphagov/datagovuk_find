# data.gov.uk team
The data.gov.uk team at the Government Digital Service (GDS) is transforming the data.gov.uk website and the experience of finding and using public sector data.

If you want to contact the team, please complete our [feedback form](https://forms.office.com/e/9V26PNFQaR) or, if you work in government, get in touch via our Slack channel: [#datagovuk](https://ukgovernmentdigital.slack.com/archives/C037J3GTE4T).
