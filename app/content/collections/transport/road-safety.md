Get data on road collisions in England, Scotland and Wales from 1979, and the vehicles and casualties involved. The statistics relate to personal injury collisions on public roads that are reported to the police. The data is published annually as a CSV file by the Department for Transport, and contains the non-sensitive fields that can be made public from road safety records.
