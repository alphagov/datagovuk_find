Get UK climate projections from the Met Office. This page provides climate model projections for the UK, showing how the climate is likely to change in the future until 2100. 

You can access high-resolution, spatially-coherent, future climate projections for the globe at 60km scale and for the UK at 12 km scale. The 12km climate model allows for realistic simulations of high-impact events such as localised heavy rainfall in summer, projections of sea-level rise and storm surges.

The data includes: 

- spreadsheets in XLSX format of results based on probabilistic and sea level projections
- maps with projections over land for different variables across UK nations, administrative regions and river basins until 2099
