Sites of Specific Scientific Interest (SSSIs) are designated areas in the UK recognised for their ecological or geological significance. SSSIs support many characteristic, rare and endangered species, habitats and natural features. Download data for SSSIs in England in various formats, or find SSSIs for an area on the interactive map.
