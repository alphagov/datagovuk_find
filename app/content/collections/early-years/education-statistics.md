Explore early years education statistics in England including:

- childcare and early years provider survey
- childcare and early years survey of parents
- early years education recovery
- early years foundation stage profile results
- early childcare entitlements
- funded early education and childcare

The Department for Education statistics service allows you to browse and view reports and headline figures, explore previous reports through a times series tab. You can download their associated data, or create custom filters for the data you are interested in as ODS or CSV files. An API is also available which allows you to automate access to statistics and data available via the service.
