Get Ofsted’s official statistics on the inspections and outcomes of state-funded schools and academies in England since 2019. The statistics are published twice a year and include:

- the main findings
- tables, charts and individual school-level data
- a quality and methodology report

There are also datasets for inspections and outcomes management information, Parent View data, transparency data, FOI requests, Five-Year Ofsted Inspection Data, and data before September 2019.
