View a wide range of datasets covering fires, false alarms and other incidents attended by fire crews. You can download each dataset as an XLSX file. This data is collected by the Ministry of Housing, Communities and Local Government. 

The statistics include the numbers of incidents, fires, fatalities and casualties, as well as information on response times to fires. Information on the workforce, fire prevention work, health and safety and firefighter pensions is also available.
