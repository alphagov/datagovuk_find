A collection of detailed time series data tables on people’s housing circumstances and the condition and energy efficiency of the English housing stock. The data is updated each year and includes: 

- parking, mains gas and EV charge points
- dwelling condition, safety and security
- energy performance, heating and insulation
- leaseholders 
- new households and recent movers
- owner occupiers, recent first-time buyers and second homes
- social and private renters
- stock profile
- tenure trends and cross tenure analysis 

These datasets are owned by the Ministry of Housing, Communities and Local Government.
