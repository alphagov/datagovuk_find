Get the latest prices for a range of agricultural commodities, including cereals, livestock, milk, eggs, animal feed, bananas, fruit and vegetables. This data is provided by the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs. The datasets are updated on a weekly or monthly basis. There are also separate collections for agricultural price indices, and the prices for egg packers, farm-gate milk and cattle compensation.
