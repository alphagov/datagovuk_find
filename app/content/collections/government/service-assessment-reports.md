Search reports of government services that were assessed against the government Service Standard. The standard was developed by the Government Digital Service and helps teams create and run effective, user-centred public services.

Filter reports by assessment date, the stage being assessed (alpha, beta, live) and assessment results (met, not met). The earliest assessment is from 2013.
