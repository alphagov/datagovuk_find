Get the latest data tables from the Ministry of Housing, Communities and Local Government on: 

- local authorities’ outstanding borrowing and investments for the UK 
- quarterly capital expenditure and receipts – at England level and by local authority 
- receipts of Council Taxes collected during a financial year in England 
- estimates of data reported by local authorities regarding exit payments 
- claimant numbers per quarter for localised Council Tax support for local authorities in England 
- quarterly revenue update, which collects information on current expenditure by local authorities for quarters 1 to 3 each financial year 

The separate statistics compendium page has annual datasets for local government finance statistics in England since 2010, available in ODS or XLS format.
