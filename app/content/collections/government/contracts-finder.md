Search the GOV.UK Contracts Finder for contracts published by central government departments, local authorities and other government agencies. You can use it to find public sector procurement opportunities or contracts already awarded.

Enter a keyword, location or procurement stage to start your search. You can filter results by additional factors such as date range, contract value or industry code, and download the results as an XML or CSV file.

The platform is free to use and does not require an account to search, but creating an account allows you to receive email updates and save searches.
