Data and statistics about the local bus sector in England, Scotland and Wales. The data tables include: 

- quarterly bus fares statistics
- local bus passenger journeys
- local bus vehicle distance travelled
- passenger distance travelled
- costs, fares and revenue
- government support for the bus industry and concessionary travel
- vehicles operated by local bus operators
- staff employed by local bus operators
- concessionary travel
- bus reliability and punctuality
- historical data downloads
- discontinued bus statistics 


This data is owned by the Department for Transport.
