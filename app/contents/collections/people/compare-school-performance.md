Compare the performance of schools, colleges and multi-academy trusts in England. Find a school by searching a name, location, local authority or parliamentary consistency. Results include the school’s details such as address and headteacher, the performance of pupils at the school and pupil absence data. Depending on the school’s education phase(s), performance data is based on results at key stage 2, key stage 4, A level qualifications and students who progressed to higher education or training.
