Details of registered and licensed fishing vessels over 10 metres and 10 metres and under. The spreadsheets list the UK fishing vessels including: 

- administrative port
- home port
- port letters and number
- vessel name
- registry of shipping and seamen number
- overall length 

This data is owned by the Marine Management Organisation.
